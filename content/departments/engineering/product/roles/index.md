## Product Manager

You **own an area of the product**, which means you:

- Define what success means for that product area.
- Figure out how to achieve that success.
- Define, communicate, and maintain the vision, strategy, and roadmap for that product area.
- Talk with users and customers daily.
- Are a power user of that product area and of our product as a whole.
